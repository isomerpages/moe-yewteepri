### School Advisory Committee

Our SAC comprises members who come from different walks of life who have come forward voluntarily to serve in YTPS. They work closely with the principal and staff, and give strong support to pupil development and school programmes; raising funds to promote school development and programmes for pupil welfare and promoting closer relationship between the community and the school.

The SAC contributes time and expertise to support school programmes such as:

* helping to establish links with the grassroots, providing financial support to the needy pupils

sharing their expertise with our pupils (Education & Career Guidance Fair, Community Projects with/for the Elderly) funding pupil awards to recognise holistic development namely:

* The Sports Boy and Girl Awards (for 2 P6 pupils who demonstrate exemplary sportsmanship and achievement)

* The Sports Talent Award (for 4 pupils from P4 and P5 who show potential in the sport and demonstrate the values of perseverance and resilience)

Supporting staff awards such as:

* The YTPS Long Service Award (for staff who have rendered 10 years or more of service to YTPS)

* My Partner-Teacher Award (for teachers who demonstrate commitment to building strong partnership with parents)
