### 2019 Art Works

#### Singapore Youth Festival Art Exhibition (2019)

The Singapore Youth Festival Art Exhibition, in its 51st year in 2019, celebrates the artist in every child. It showcases children’s creative ideas, personal aspirations and hard work put into artistic expressions. This year, the exhibition features artworks by primary schools, full schools (primary section), Sped Schools (Primary) and Singapore International School Hong Kong. In line with the 2018 Primary Art Syllabus, the exhibition is curated around three focus areas of the syllabus, namely “Self and Immediate Environment”, “Singapore Past, Present and Future” and “The World and Region We Live in”.

Click&nbsp;[here](https://www.syf.gov.sg/art-exhibition/about-the-2019-exhibition) **broken link** &nbsp;to catch the artworks of our P2 Budding Artists (Category A) and members of the Artists’ Hub (Category B and C) in the online catalogue.
